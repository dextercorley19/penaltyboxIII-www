Charter Information

Reserve

Reserve your dates through PenaltyBoxIII’s reservation portal with Northwest Explorations. Booking options iclude either accompanying NWE on a guided expidition or a week long personal charter. Whether you’re a seasoned vet or new to boating, trainings are avaliable to enable your freedom.

Plan

Bareboat
Once you’ve reserved your spot, it’s time to create your float plan! We encourage you to browse some of our favorite destinations listed above or speak with a team member at NWE. Though the PNW has no shortage of beautiful desitnations, its good to have a float plan.
Floatilla
If you’re choosing to attend one of the floatillas (Alaska, Desloation Sound, or Princess Louisa) the crew at NWE will have your float plan covered. They’ll take care of watching the tides, weather, and charting your course. The team also includes some wildlife scienctists & fishermen who lead excursions while on the trip.

Embark

When the time finally comes to embark of your voyage make sure you have a float plan complete with weather & tides consideration, relevant marina bookings, and of course; your crew. Northwest explorations will provide adequate training sessions based on need when you arrive. Once you bring in the lines from the dock, you’re off!
